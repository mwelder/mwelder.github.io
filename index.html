About Me

Welcome! My name is Morgan Elder. I'm a junior at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor, majoring in Performing Arts Technology at the School of Music, Theatre & Dance and minoring in Computer Science. I have a passion for the intersections between audio and technology, especially those dealing with interactive user experiences like soundtracks and sound design for video games or VR applications.

I graduated high school summa cum laude from the International Academy in Bloomfield Hills, MI with an International Baccalaureate (IB) degree.

When I'm not working on audio projects, you can often find me improving my programming skills, practicing Spanish and Japanese, or enjoying a video game or movie.

I will be continually updating this website to practice web programming.
