About Me

Welcome! My name is Morgan Elder. I am a sophomore at the University of Michigan in the School of Music, Theatre & Dance majoring in Performing Arts Technology with a concentration in audio for multimedia. I have a passion for the intersections between audio and technology, especially when dealing with interactive user experiences like soundtracks and sound design for video games or VR applications.

When I'm not working on audio projects, you can often find me improving my programming skills, practicing Spanish and Japanese, or enjoying a video game or movie.
